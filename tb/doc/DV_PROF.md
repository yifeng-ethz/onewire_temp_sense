# onewire_master_controller DV — Performance / Stress / Soak Cases

**Companion docs:** [DV_PLAN.md](DV_PLAN.md), [DV_HARNESS.md](DV_HARNESS.md), [DV_BASIC.md](DV_BASIC.md), [DV_EDGE.md](DV_EDGE.md), [DV_ERROR.md](DV_ERROR.md), [DV_CROSS.md](DV_CROSS.md), [DV_REPORT.md](DV_REPORT.md), [BUG_HISTORY.md](BUG_HISTORY.md)
**Parent:** [DV_PLAN.md](DV_PLAN.md)
**ID Range:** `P001-P130`
**Total:** 130 cases (0 implemented / 0 waived)

This bucket profiles sustained-load throughput, ticket-lock fairness, IRQ scaling, and Read-ROM probe stress for the six-line FEB DS18B20 monitor path. Targets are: (a) per-line >= 1.05 transactions/s and aggregate >= 6.5 transactions/s under six-line saturation; (b) ticket_lock.gnt strictly one-hot and per-line grant share within +/-5% of equal; (c) WAITING/ERROR holds matching the 850_001/1_000_001 us thresholds with no deadlock; (d) Read-ROM probes succeeding under live temperature loops on other lines without CRC errors. Long soaks (`prof_long_soak` mode) hold the bus for 30-60 s of sim time per case.

**Methodology key:**
- **D** = Directed (hand-crafted stress sequence, deterministic)
- **R** = Constrained-random (LCG-based PRNG; full Mentor license enables SV `rand` and constrained-random)
